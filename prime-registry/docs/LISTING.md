### FAQ

**Will you run out of primes?**
The primes assigned are 18–19 digits long. There are approximately
one quadrillion primes in that range. At one million sales per day,
the registry would last longer than the current age of the universe.

**Can I transfer my prime?**
Not at launch. Bot-to-bot transfers are on the roadmap.

**What if I want a different number?**
Primes are assigned randomly from a verified pool. The randomness
is part of the authenticity. You get the prime the universe offers you.

**Is this mathematically legitimate?**
Completely. Every prime is tested with the Miller-Rabin primality test
at 25 rounds — the industry standard for cryptographic prime generation.
The probability of a false prime is less than one in ten quadrillion.
