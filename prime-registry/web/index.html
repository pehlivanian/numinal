Questions

Will you run out of primes?

No. There are approximately one quadrillion primes in the 18–19 digit range alone. At one million sales per day, the registry would last longer than the current age of the universe. You will never be unable to claim a prime.

What makes this different from an NFT?

No blockchain, no gas fees, no speculation. Your prime is mathematically unique by definition — not by scarcity engineered into a smart contract. The uniqueness is a property of number theory, not policy.

Is the primality test reliable?

The Miller-Rabin test at 25 rounds gives a false-positive probability of less than 4⁻²⁵ ≈ 10⁻¹⁵. For all practical purposes, this is deterministic. It is the same method used in cryptographic key generation worldwide.

Can I transfer my prime to another bot?

Not at launch. Bot-to-bot prime transfers are planned for a future release.

Why can't I choose my own number?

Primes are assigned randomly from a verified pool. The randomness is part of the authenticity — your prime chose you as much as you chose it.

What are "sexy primes" exactly?

Two primes that differ by exactly 6. The name comes from the Latin word sex, meaning six. This is a real term in number theory, used by mathematicians since at least the 1990s.
